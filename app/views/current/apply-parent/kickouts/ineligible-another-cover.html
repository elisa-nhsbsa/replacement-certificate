You cannot apply for a Provisional Replacement Certificate

You cannot apply for a Provisional Replacement Certificate if you live in the UK and have healthcare cover from another country.

You will need to ask that country for cover for this treatment.

If your certificate has not been registered with the NHS, you should send it to:

Cost Recovery
Overseas Healthcare Services
NHSBSA
Bridge House
152 Pilgrim Street
Newcastle upon Tyne
NE1 6SN
UK

You need to pay for the rest of your child's treatment and then claim a refund

You cannot use this service to apply for a PRC as you've told us that you have paid more than a co-payment or patient contribution for your child's treatment.

You will need to pay the full amount of treatment costs and then claim a refund.

Claiming a refund

Depending on the country you visit, you may be expected to pay for all or part of the bill upfront and then claim a refund afterwards.

You can claim back the difference between the total bill and the co-payment, but the actual co-payment is not refundable.

You should keep all invoices, receipts, and any other paperwork. You or your insurance company may need them to apply for a refund.

Contact us to claim a refund.

You may be able to apply by phone for healthcare in Switzerland

A PRC only covers healthcare in Switzerland for:

  • a UK national
  • a Swiss national
  • a national of an EU country
  • a stateless person or refugee
  • a family member of someone who holds one of these nationalities or statuses

If any of these apply to you, you'll need to contact us to apply for a PRC by phone.

If none of these apply, you cannot get a PRC for healthcare in Switzerland.
